Vison



Inception of Balajee Group created foreseeing the vast infrastructure opportunity that is emerging in India in coming years. In this direction initially mining related activities were started and to widen the area’s operation Balajee entered into earthwork activities. Balajee slowly & steadily spread its’ activities throughout India to provide services infrastructure activities up to construction of road. To make Balajee’s dream come true, the company inducted sophisticated & critical equipment’s that needed to execute works like Crushing, Supply of Aggregates, GSB, WMM, DBM & BC. Today Balajee is proud in owning complete equipment’s that is needed to execute Road Project to satisfy the requirement of clients. Today, Balajee is inspired to provide Best Standard of Services in Infrastructure and related activities within framed deadline by utilizing knowledge, expertise and competence of Professionally & Technically qualified Team of Balajee and thereby contributing to the growth of the Nation and the masses with Safe and Healthy working Environment.

  • Provide professional, time efficient and cost effective solutions on a national basis
  • High level engagement with our clients to provide a personal service
  • Deliver solutions that combine innovation and best industrial practice
  • Maintain and develop outstanding people in a values driven culture
  • Be a responsible neighbor in all the communities in which we operate
  • To become the industry leading partner of choice for infrastructure construction solutions



Values


Integrity is the foundation of a good name and an essential ingredient for true success in life and in business. We seek to operate according to the highest standards of integrity and in a manner consistent with basic principles.

By providing the quality works at an exceptional value and to complete our projects in a timely, efficient and organized manner. We thrive to continue to learn and improve as we endeavor for excellence in every area.
We are committed to serve our clients as a unified team in order to ensure the success of each project. We involve totally in each job and willingly go to the extra mile to exceed expectations of our clients. We treat each job as an opportunity to proactively find creative solutions for our client’s needs.
We value our clients, employees, subcontractors and vendors. We relate to our staffs, co-workers and clients in an honest and trustworthy manner. We respect each person as an individual and we treat others as we would want to be treated.
By creating an atmosphere where employees grow spiritually, personally and professionally and families take priority over business. We value integrity, commitment and teamwork from our employees. Each person is rewarded for their excellent performance and for their contribution to the team.
