Recruitment Policy

In Balajee we ensure that the company attracts and retains the best of professionals available in each of its areas of functioning and provide career plan and succession plan while meeting the growth need of our business and meet the aspiration levels of employees. To focus on the placement of employees in jobs to which they are best fitted physically, mentally and temperamentally, where they have strong expectations of being well adjusted to their work and to the working environment

Life in Balajee

Balajee provides ample working atmosphere that is always open. The open environment that is in existence fosters the exchange of new ideas and views across the organization which facilitates flow of information. To express opinion, the staffs do not see the need to wait for a forum or a platform. There is always space for diversity of opinions and point of views and a person’s position is not the criteria in the organization. Always there is scope for improvement in the organization.

Corporate Governance

C.S.R

Balajee Infratech and Constructions Private Limited (“The Company”) considers Corporate Social Responsibility as a process for enhancing the lives of the community in which it operates and strongly believes in contributing towards social welfare of the people and the betterment of the society/community at large, more specifically, for the deprived and the under privileged people.

H.S.E

Balajee Infratech & Constructions Private Limited believes that every individual employee of the Company is Wealth & Prosperity Creator for each other. Hence the Company believes the truth lying and truth lying in that all accidents can be prevented & no tasks are so important that risk of injury to mankind or damage to the environment is justified.

Q.A

The Company is committed to operate every aspect of Infrastructure activities to the highest standards that offer the highest possible quality of service to all our Clientele. This is supported by a progressive Management style that encourages the presence and feel of Quality culture throughout the Company.
